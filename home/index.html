Scalable Vector Graphics

Are text-based images. They are rendered by the browser and are made of XML.

How is SVG different?

  Unlike raster images, which are pixel-based, SVG's are point-based. Rather than having a fixed amount of space, like the pixel, svg points can be scaled inifinetley because they are plotted on a cartesian grid.

Things that you can use to make SVG's

Inkscape, Illustrator, Sketch, GIMP, or even A text editor.
